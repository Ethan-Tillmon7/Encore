What does the simplified tab bar look like?

The home screen replaces Discover. The question is what tabs survive alongside it.

A

Home · Lineup · Schedule · Map

Home = dashboard (group, travel, packing, expenses)
Lineup = day-by-day browse of all festival artists
Schedule = your personal picks + conflict detection
Map = stage map (keep as-is)

Crew is folded into Home (group card). Discover is replaced by Lineup. 4 tabs total.

B

Home · Lineup · Map

Home = dashboard + your personal schedule lives here too (below the cards)
Lineup = day-by-day browse of all artists, tap to add to schedule
Map = stage map

Crew and Schedule collapse into Home. Most aggressive simplification. 3 tabs total.

C

Home · Lineup · Crew · Map

Home = dashboard (group, travel, packing, expenses) + your schedule at bottom
Lineup = day-by-day browse of all artists
Crew = crew schedule, member presence, meetup pins
Map = stage map

Schedule is embedded in Home. Crew keeps its own tab. 4 tabs total.
